Contact Mayor Dewey

Got a question, concern, or just want to say “hello”? Mayor Dewey (that's Mayor Nanefua Dewey these days!) is here to help! Whether you're curious about town events, safety guidelines, or Beach City's rich (and occasionally bizarre) history, our mayor's office is always open. Drop by City Hall, leave a note in the suggestion box, or tune in to Dewey's weekly radio update. At Beach City, we believe in community—and our mayor is just a wave away!
