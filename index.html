Welcome to Beach City!

Beach City

Beach City is a cozy seaside town filled with sun, surf, and surprises! Whether you're here to relax on the boardwalk, grab a bite at the Big Donut, or maybe catch a glimpse of the Crystal Gems, you're in for a unique experience!

Fun Fact “Did you know? Beach City was the site of a giant robot battle... more than once!”

Come and Explore

Looking for fun in Beach City? There's always something to do! Spend the day soaking up the sun on the beach, then grab a snack at the Big Donut or Fish Stew Pizza. Take a stroll along the boardwalk to play arcade games, browse quirky shops, or catch a glimpse of the Crystal Gems near the mysterious temple carved into the cliffside. Whether you're building sandcastles, stargazing at night, or joining in a town festival, Beach City is full of charm, adventure, and a little bit of magic!
